FAQs

What kind of products and services do you offer?

At SPORTEX, we specialize in providing a comprehensive range of sports and fitness products to help you stay active and healthy. From premium workout apparel and durable fitness equipment to yoga accessories and specialized training programs, we aim to deliver everything you need to power your fitness goals. Our products are carefully selected for quality, comfort, and performance, ensuring you get the best support throughout your journey.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We are proud to offer international shipping to many countries around the world. Shipping rates and estimated delivery times will vary depending on your location. For full details, please refer to our Shipping Policy page.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and security. All transactions on our website are protected, secure payment gateways with end-to-end encryption, ensuring your personal and financial information is fully protected.

Can I modify or cancel my order after purchase?

If your order hasn't yet been processed or shipped, we're happy to assist with changes or cancellations. Please contact our support team as soon as possible, and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

We process most orders within 1 to 2 business days. After your order has been shipped, standard delivery typically takes 3 to 7 business days, depending on your location. Please note that shipping times may be slightly longer during holidays or peak periods. You'll receive a tracking number once your order is on its way from our warehouse to your doorstep.

Returns & Refund

What is your return and refund policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. We offer a 30-day return window on most items. Products must be returned in unused, original condition with all packaging intact. To begin a return or refund request, simply visit our Returns & Refunds page or contact our support team. Please be aware that certain items may not be eligible for return due to hygiene and safety standards, like mats or worn gear.

Order & Tracking

How can I track my order?

Tracking your order is easy! As soon as your package ships, you'll receive an email with your tracking details and a direct link to follow its progress. You can also log in to your SPORTEX account anytime to view your current order status and shipping updates.
