Privacy & Policy

At SPORTEX, your privacy matters. We collect only the necessary information required to process your orders, improve your shopping experience, and keep you updated only if you opt in. We never sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except when required to fulfill your order or comply with legal obligations. We use advanced encryption and security protocols to keep your information safe.

We reserve the right to update these terms as necessary. Please review them periodically to stay informed.

Warranty & Returns Policy

We stand by every product we sell. Most items are backed by a 30-day return policy. If your item is defective or arrives damaged, we'll replace it or offer a full refund no hassle.

To be eligible for a return or warranty claim, items must be unused and in their original packaging. Hygiene-sensitive items (such as worn apparel or used mats) may not be returnable. For full details, check our Returns & Refund page or contact customer service.
